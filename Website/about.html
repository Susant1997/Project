About the Business

Art in Motion has started in Late 2019 in the heart of Tauranga. AlM is aimed to find people who are talented and interested: to dance and to support them to grow. AiM has 3 forms of dance (Bharatanatyam,; Mohiniyattam and Hip-Hop) and along with that AiM provides Yoga and Meditation sessions: Where Barathnatyam, and Mohiniyattam are two south indian classical dances. Aim wanted to grow the culture and spread the art form so that it won't be gone away.
